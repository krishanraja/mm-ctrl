# Purpose

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-20

## What Mindmaker Is

Mindmaker is a portable AI context platform that gives leaders a **digital double** — a living, exportable knowledge base about who they are, what they do, and how they think. It makes every AI tool a leader uses (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor) instantly personalized.

It is also an AI literacy system that helps senior leaders build the mental operating system they need to stay sharp, skeptical, and in control as Gen AI reshapes work, value, and power.

It **is**:
- A voice-first platform that builds a Memory Web from natural conversation
- A portable context export system that works across any AI tool
- AI-powered thinking tools (Decision Advisor, Meeting Prep, Prompt Coach) that already know the leader's world
- A diagnostic system that maps how leaders think and decide when AI is involved
- A pattern detection engine that surfaces strengths to amplify and blind spots to address
- Infrastructure for better AI interactions — not another AI tool

It is **not**:
- An AI implementation platform
- A tool selection advisor
- A ChatGPT wrapper or quiz product
- Generic leadership coaching with AI branding
- A hype session or prompt tricks collection
- Locked to any single AI provider

## Core Problem Statement

**Every leader uses AI. But every AI conversation starts from zero.**

The AI never knows who you are, what your company does, what you're working on, or what decisions you're facing. So the advice stays generic. Leaders who get real value from AI have figured out how to give it context — but doing that manually across multiple tools is time-consuming and requires prompt engineering skills most leaders don't have.

**The deeper problem — AI literacy gap:**
- Vendors and staff overselling things leaders can't properly challenge
- Competitors quietly building leverage while others sit through decks
- Decisions being made about AI without leaders trusting their own view

**What leaders need:**
1. AI that knows them — portable context that travels across every tool
2. AI literacy — the ability to walk into AI conversations with clarity
3. Thinking tools — mental models for decisions, not prompt tricks

## How Mindmaker Solves This

1. **Talk naturally** — Voice or text, no prompt engineering
2. **Memory Web builds** — AI extracts structured facts about identity, business, goals, challenges, preferences
3. **Export anywhere** — One click to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, Claude Code
4. **Use built-in tools** — Decision Advisor, Meeting Prep, Prompt Coach already know your context
5. **Get smarter over time** — Pattern detection, 10X skills mapping, adaptive recommendations

## Three Audiences, One System

### 1. Leaders (Individual Executives)
**Job to be Done**: Build a portable AI double that makes every AI tool personalized, and develop AI literacy to maintain control and credibility

**Context**:
- C-suite executives, VPs, senior managers
- Time-poor, skeptical of hype
- Already using AI tools but getting generic results
- Need context portability, not another tool to learn

### 2. Exec Teams (Leadership Teams)
**Job to be Done**: Align team mental models and decision frameworks around AI

**Context**:
- Leadership offsites, strategy sprints
- Need shared language and frameworks
- Working through real decisions together
- Building pilot charters and provocation reports

### 3. Partners (VCs, PE, Consultants, SIs)
**Job to be Done**: Assess portfolio AI readiness and identify intervention opportunities

**Context**:
- Managing multiple portfolio companies
- Need consistent assessment framework
- Identifying co-delivery opportunities
- Prioritizing intervention resources

## Strategic Positioning

**What We Enable**:
- Every AI tool a leader uses becomes personalized instantly
- Portable context that isn't locked to any platform
- Better capital allocation decisions
- Cleaner conversations with boards and vendors
- Less wasted AI spend
- More credible internal AI leadership

**What We Don't Do**:
- Vendor selection or tool procurement
- Change management or implementation
- Generic executive education
- AI-powered automation services

## Measurement of Success

- Time to first export (<2 minutes)
- Number of AI tools receiving exported context
- Leader ability to challenge AI proposals with specific questions
- Quality improvement in AI interactions (personalized vs generic)
- Reduction in "AI theatre" spend
- Increase in AI literacy-based decision quality over time
