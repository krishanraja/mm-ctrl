# Purpose

## What Mindmaker Is

Mindmaker is an AI literacy system that helps senior leaders build the mental operating system they need to stay sharp, sceptical, and in control as Gen AI reshapes work, value, and power.

It is **not**:
- An AI implementation platform
- A tool selection advisor
- A ChatGPT wrapper or quiz product
- Generic leadership coaching with AI branding
- A hype session or prompt tricks collection

It **is**:
- A diagnostic system that maps how leaders think and decide when AI is involved
- A framework for surfacing tensions, risks, and blind spots
- Infrastructure for workshops and strategic sessions
- A provider of mental models, questions, and habits for working alongside AI

## Core Problem Statement

Senior leaders face a literacy gap, not an implementation gap:

**The Risk**: 
- Vendors and staff overselling things leaders can't properly challenge
- Competitors quietly building leverage while others sit through decks
- Decisions being made about AI without leaders trusting their own view

**The Need**:
Leaders need to walk into AI conversations with clarity instead of hoping they're not missing something.

## Three Audiences, One System

### 1. Leaders (Individual Executives)
**Job to be Done**: Upgrade personal AI literacy to maintain control and credibility

**Context**: 
- C-suite executives, VPs, senior managers
- Time-poor, sceptical of hype
- Need to challenge vendors, boards, and internal teams
- Want to use AI as thinking partner without outsourcing judgement

### 2. Exec Teams (Leadership Teams)
**Job to be Done**: Align team mental models and decision frameworks around AI

**Context**:
- Leadership offsites, strategy sprints
- Need shared language and frameworks
- Working through real decisions together
- Building pilot charters and provocation reports

### 3. Partners (VCs, PE, Consultants, SIs)
**Job to be Done**: Assess portfolio AI readiness and identify intervention opportunities

**Context**:
- Managing multiple portfolio companies
- Need consistent assessment framework
- Identifying co-delivery opportunities
- Prioritising intervention resources

## Strategic Positioning

**What We Enable**:
- Better capital allocation decisions
- Cleaner conversations with boards and vendors
- Less wasted AI spend
- More credible internal AI leadership
- Structural preparedness for agents and automation

**What We Don't Do**:
- Vendor selection or tool procurement
- Change management or implementation
- Generic executive education
- AI-powered automation services

## Measurement of Success

Success is measured by:
- Leader ability to challenge AI proposals with specific questions
- Team alignment on AI decision criteria and risk tolerance
- Partner confidence in portfolio intervention prioritisation
- Reduction in "AI theatre" spend across client organisations
- Increase in AI literacy-based decision quality over time
