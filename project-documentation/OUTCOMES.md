# User Outcomes

Expected outcomes and success metrics for each audience.

---

## Leaders Tool Outcomes

### Immediate Outcomes (Day 1)

**Cognitive Clarity**
- Clear understanding of personal AI literacy level
- Awareness of specific gaps and blind spots
- Concrete mental model for "where I stand"

**Actionable Next Steps**
- 3-5 specific thinking tools ready to use immediately
- Questions to ask vendors, boards, and teams
- Daily AI usage patterns identified

**Emotional Shift**
- Reduced anxiety about "falling behind"
- Increased confidence in AI conversations
- Scepticism transformed into constructive questioning

**Measurable**:
- 90%+ report "now know where I stand" (post-diagnostic survey)
- 3+ thinking tools used in first week (usage tracking)
- 50%+ report increased confidence in AI discussions (30-day follow-up)

### 30-Day Outcomes

**Behavioural Change**
- Daily AI tool usage for analysis or decision-making
- Challenging at least 1 AI proposal with specific questions
- Using mental models in team discussions

**Decision Quality**
- More informed AI investment decisions
- Reduced spend on low-ROI AI initiatives
- Clearer vendor evaluation criteria

**Organisational Impact**
- Team members asking sharper AI questions
- Reduced "AI theatre" projects initiated
- More realistic AI pilot scoping

**Measurable**:
- 70%+ using AI daily as thinking partner (self-report)
- 60%+ challenged or redirected an AI initiative (case study)
- 40%+ reduction in low-value AI spend (budget review)

### 90-Day Outcomes

**Strategic Capability**
- Leading AI literacy conversations in leadership team
- Contributing meaningfully to board AI discussions
- Mentoring direct reports on AI thinking

**Structural Preparedness**
- Understanding of agent impact on role/team
- Proactive workflow redesign discussions
- Governance frameworks in place

**Competitive Advantage**
- Faster identification of AI opportunities
- Better risk assessment of AI-driven threats
- Stronger positioning in AI-related negotiations

**Measurable**:
- 50%+ led AI discussion in leadership meeting (manager confirmation)
- 40%+ initiated workflow redesign conversation (documentation)
- 30%+ report competitive advantage gained (business impact)

## Cross-Tool Success Indicators

### Qualitative Indicators

**Language Shift**
- "I can now challenge AI proposals"
- "My team has a shared framework"
- "I know which questions to ask"
- "I'm using AI as a thinking partner"
- "We stopped a bad AI project early"

### Quantitative Indicators

**Engagement Metrics**
- Diagnostic completion rate >80%
- Workshop attendance rate >90%
- Post-diagnostic tool usage >70% in first week

**Impact Metrics**
- AI spend efficiency improvement (tracked via budget)
- Decision cycle time reduction (tracked via meeting minutes)
- Competitive positioning improvement (tracked via market share)

**Retention Metrics**
- Repeat workshop bookings from teams
- Referral rate from leaders to peers
- Partner tool usage frequency

### Anti-Metrics (What We Don't Measure)

**Avoid**:
- Number of prompts generated (vanity metric)
- Time spent in tool (quality > quantity)
- Quiz scores as end goal (diagnostic, not exam)
- Feature usage breadth (focused outcomes > feature bloat)
