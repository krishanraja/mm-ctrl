## How It Works

1. **Talk naturally** — Describe your work, goals, challenges. Voice or text.
2. **We build your Memory Web** — AI extracts structured facts about you: identity, business context, objectives, blockers, preferences.
3. **Export to any AI tool** — One click. Your context is formatted for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Cursor, or any LLM.
4. **Every AI interaction is personalized** — From the first word. No re-explaining.

The more you use it, the smarter every AI tool gets about you.
